Can I use Ex Prūnīs with my other products?

Yes. Ex Prūnīs works wonderfully with day and night creams. Just add a couple of drops to your favorite day or night cream or a moisturizer to boost their nourishing properties and to add the EX PRŪNĪS signature scent.

How fast will I see the results?

Oleic acid can instantly make your skin feel smoother. For this reason, many notice a difference overnight. However, only frequent use can lead to a significant decrease in the appearance of fine lines and sunspots.

For what skin type is the Ex Prūnīs best suited?

EX PRŪNĪS works wonders on all skin types. It is well-suited for dry and mature skin, but as a non-comedogenic oil, it works beautifully also on acne-prone skin. Free of essential oils and any additives, it is well-suited for ultra-sensitive, dehydrated, as well as allergy-prone skin.

Is it safe to use during pregnancy?

Each fresh batch of EX PRŪNĪS Antioxidant Treatment Oil undergoes a strict analysis of its active ingredients with tight control of undesirable contaminants that are present in the modern environment such as heavy metals. The aforementioned goes hand in hand with our decision to keep EX PRŪNĪS free from essential oils, some of which might not be safe for use during pregnancy. EX PRŪNĪS is 100% pure and free from any additives.

What is the shelf life?

Once opened, we recommend using the oil within six months to enjoy most of its natural properties. However, the condition of storing is crucial for the preservation of the active ingredients within and its properties. Keep EX PRŪNĪS away from direct sunlight and away from extreme temperatures and always tightly closed when not in use.

Is it tested on animals?

No. European legislation does not allow any testing on animals, which is the reason why it is against the law to state that a cosmetic brand does not carry out such tests, including the cruelty-free label. In our raw material selection, the welfare of animals is critical for our decisions. It is one of the reasons why we chose organic ingredients intending to prevent any use of synthetic chemicals that can lead to toxicity in pollinating insects and additional adverse impact on the habitat and local biodiversity.
